About the Puppies

Look at these precious faces! We have both male and female puppies available, each one raised in a loving home filled with the laughter of children—making them the perfect addition to any family. Our puppies have been nurtured with Early Neurological Stimulation (ENS) and Early Scent Introduction (ESI), ensuring they grow into well-rounded and confident companions.

Both parents have been thoroughly health tested, giving you peace of mind that your new furry friend is as healthy as they are cute! The puppies are up to date on vaccines and have received a clean bill of health from our trusted veterinarian.

These little ones are expected to weigh between 20 to 25 lbs as adults, making them the perfect size for snuggles and adventures alike. Don't miss out on the chance to bring home one of these bundles of joy—each one is ready to fill your life with love and happiness!
